Who We Are

born in
the dust

We are disko dolce — a music collective born from the desert heat of AfrikaBurn 2024, where a group of strangers became a family.

What started as a theme camp, a shared love for freedom and music under a boundless sky, ignited something that refused to stay on the playa. We believe in dolce vita — in sweetness, in connection, in the power of music to dissolve boundaries and bring bodies and spirits together.

In 2025, we formed our collective to carry that burning spirit beyond the dust. We are not just a DJ collective. We are a community — one that shows up for each other, that turns a night out into an experience, that brings not only good music but a rare and special crowd into every space we inhabit.

When you step into a disko dolce night, you feel the difference. This is disko dolce. This is disko dolce music.
